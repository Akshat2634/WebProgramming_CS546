My Travel Destinations

San Francisco, CA

San Francisco is a vibrant city with a rich cultural history and diverse neighborhoods. The iconic Golden Gate Bridge, the bustling Fisherman's Wharf, and the winding Lombard Street are just a few of the city's many attractions.

My favorite places to visit in San Francisco are:

  1. Golden Gate Bridge
  2. Alcatraz Island
  3. Chinatown

New York, NY

New York, also known as "The Big Apple", is a city that never sleeps. From the towering skyscrapers of Manhattan to the hipster vibes of Brooklyn, there's always something to see and do.

My favorite places to visit in New York are:

  1. Central Park
  2. Times Square
  3. The Metropolitan Museum of Art

Washington DC

Washington DC is the capital of the United States and home to some of the country's most famous landmarks, including the White House, the Capitol Building, and the Lincoln Memorial.

My favorite places to visit in Washington DC are:

  1. The White House
  2. The National Mall
  3. The Smithsonian Institution
